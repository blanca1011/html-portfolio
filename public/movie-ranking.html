The Best Movies According to Azul

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Flipped

Two eighth-graders start to have feelings for each other despite being total opposites.

The Legend of 1900

A baby boy discovered on an ocean liner in 1900 grows into a musical prodigy, never setting foot on land.

Coco

Aspiring musician Miguel, confronted with his family's ancestral ban on music, enters the Land of the Dead to find his great-great-grandfather, a legendary singer.
